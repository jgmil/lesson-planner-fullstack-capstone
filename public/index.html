My journal

The Present Mind is a mindfulness journal that allows you to track your mindfulness practice by helping you set intentions, track your mood and meditations, reflect on your practice and log daily gratitudes.

Mindfulness is about being of aware of your thoughts, feeelings, emotions, and surroundings with a sense of curiousity, without judging or striving to change them.

According to mindful.org, "Mindfulness is the basic human ability to be fully present, aware of where we are and what we’re doing, and not overly reactive or overwhelmed by what’s going on around us."

Mindfulness and meditation has shown to have significant benefits. According to the American Psychological Association, mindfulness reduces stress, promotes positive emotional health, improves focus, decreases emotional reactivity and increases relationships satisfaction. Evidence also suggests that it improves physical health and immune functioning.
